Pray for Spiritual Awakening in the Churches in your City

Pray the Scriptural prayers found in Eph 1:15-21, 3:14-21; Phil 1:9-11; Col 1:9-12, 4:12; and Heb 13:20-21. When you pray these Scriptures, insert the name of congregations and assemblies in your city, asking God to bring the fullness of Jesus’ life into the church.

Pray for Our Leaders

Pray for people such as the Mayor, the City Council, Police Chief, School Board, etc. It's not easy running a city and these position have heavy burdens to bear. Our leaders need our support. We can honor our leaders through prayer.

Pray for our Police

Right now the Police in our city need prayer. Pray for safety, hope, and that people will support our police officers so they can continue to serve and protect our city

Pray Against the Spiritual Strongholds of Darkness over your City.

Ephesians 6:12 teaches of the constant spiritual battle in the heaven lies.. actual strongholds that the enemy has over geographic areas. (see also Dan 10:10-13). Command the “rulers, authorities, powers of this dark world, and the spiritual forces of evil” in the north, south, east, and west of your city to “give up” those people held in spiritual bondage (see Is 43:5,6). Praise the Lord that people will come to Jesus from the four sections of your city. (Lk 13:29)
